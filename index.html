Why Hire a Co-op Student?
🎯

Pre-Vetted Talent Pipeline

Access motivated, academically rigorous students from one of the nation's top co-op programs — at no recruiting fee.

📅

Flexible, Full-Time Commitment

Students work 35–40 hrs/week for 4–6 months, giving you real project output — not a shadow or a short stint.

🤝

Ongoing University Support

Dedicated co-op advisors manage logistics, handle academic credits, and support both you and the student throughout.
