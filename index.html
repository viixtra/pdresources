Why Hire a D'Amore-McKim Student?

D'Amore-McKim undergraduates are equipped with skills that align with the needs of modern business — including data analysis, technology applications, cultural agility, and an entrepreneurial mindset. Before their first day, every student completes a required co-op preparatory course and is ready to contribute from the start.

Nurture Future Talent

Identify potential full-time hires early. Students bring up to 18 months of industry experience and a strong motivation to convert.

Flexible Terms

Students work full-time for 4 to 8 months across fall, spring, and summer cycles — long enough to contribute to meaningful projects.

Prepared on Day One

Every student completes a required co-op prep course covering professional skills, workplace expectations, and industry readiness.

Diverse Perspectives

Our students bring varied backgrounds and experiences that foster innovation, creativity, and a more inclusive work environment.

Supportive Partnership

Dedicated co-op faculty understand employer needs, offer recruiting guidance, and are available throughout the entire term.

Cost Effective

Co-op students are paid hourly with no recruiting fees and no agency markups — a flexible, affordable way to meet your hiring needs.

When a student is on co-op, that is their sole responsibility. Students focus exclusively on full-time work — 35 to 40 hours per week for 4 to 8 months — so they can truly immerse themselves in your organization.

Is budget a barrier to getting started?

A potential funding resource may be available for first-time co-op employers or organizations with a lapse in hiring of two or more cycles. Eligibility is subject to review and is not guaranteed. Reach out to our team directly to learn more and see if your organization qualifies.
